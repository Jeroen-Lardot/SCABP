The importance of statistics

Why is statistics so impotant in every branch of modern science?

March 19, 2021 Chenjian Wang ARTICLE

Statistics uses numerical evidence to draw valid conclusions. Statistics allow us to evaluate claims based on quantitative evidence and help differentiate between reasonable and dubious conclusions. That aspect is particularly vital because data are so plentiful along with interpretations presented by people with unknown motivations. 

Statistical analyses account for uncertainty and error in the results. Statisticians ensure that all aspects of a study follow the appropriate methods to produce trustworthy results. These methods are: producing reliable data; analyzing the data appropriately; drawing reasonable conclusions.
